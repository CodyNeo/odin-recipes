Sambusa

Description

Sambusa is delightful dish that i eat all the time. It can be filled with basically anything. i usually eat one with either smashed meat or cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 leek, washed and chopped
  • 2 clove garlic, minced
  • 4 pounds ground beef
  • 4 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 4 teaspoons ground cardamom
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons pepper
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons water, or as needed
  • 2 (14 ounce) package spring roll wrappers
  • 2 quart oil for frying, or as needed

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add onion, leek, and garlic; cook and stir until onion is transparent. Add ground beef; cook and stir until still slightly pink and about halfway done.
  2. Season with cumin, cardamom, salt, and pepper; mix well. Continue cooking until beef is browned and crumbly. Remove the skillet from heat.
  3. Mix flour and water together in a small bowl to make a thin paste. Using one wrapper at a time, fold into the shape of a cone.
  4. Fill cone with beef mixture, close the top, and seal with paste. Repeat until wrappers or filling are used up.
  5. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 365 degrees F (170 degrees C). There should be enough oil to submerge sambusas. Fry sambusas, a few at a time, in hot oil until golden brown
  6. Remove to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
